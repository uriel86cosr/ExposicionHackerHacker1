¿What is a hacker?

A hacker is a person with advanced skills in the use of computers and technology, who uses their knowledge to explore and improve computer systems. The term "hacker" has both positive and negative connotations, depending on the context. Some hackers are dedicated to creating software and tools that improve the security or efficiency of systems, while others use their skills to breach the security of other people's systems for criminal or malicious purposes. It is important to distinguish between ethical hackers, who are dedicated to improving system security and detecting vulnerabilities, and malicious hackers, who use their skills for illegal or harmful purposes.

¿ Common characteristics of hackers ?



Curiosity: Hackers have great curiosity and a need to understand how systems and technology work.

Logical thinking: Hackers are able to analyze systems and find creative solutions to problems.

Advanced computer skills: Hackers have a deep understanding of computer systems, programming, networks, and computer security.

Creativity: Hackers have an ability to think creatively and find solutions to complex problems.

Self-taught: Hackers are often self-taught and take a self-taught approach to acquiring knowledge.
